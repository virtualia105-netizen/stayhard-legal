Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026
Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data Stay Hard collects, how it is used, and the controls you have over it. We handle sensitive personal information including habit tracking and addiction data with the highest level of care.

For any privacy-related questions, data requests, or account deletion requests, please contact us at the email above. We respond within 5 business days.

2. Data We Collect

When you use Stay Hard, we collect the following categories of data:

  • Account information: Name, username, and profile data provided during registration via OAuth (Sign in with Apple or Google).
  • Discipline Score and progress: Your personal score, streak counts, discipline ratings, and historical performance data.
  • Habit data: The habits you track, your completion logs, frequency, and custom habit configurations.
  • Addiction and streak data: Addiction types you choose to track and your sobriety/streak records. This is sensitive data covered in Section 4.
  • Verification photos: Photos submitted for AI-based habit verification. Processed transiently and not stored permanently. See Section 5.
  • Combat data: Challenge records, matchmaking history, battle results, and leaderboard rankings.
  • Device and usage data: Device type, OS version, app version, and anonymized crash reports.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate and improve the Stay Hard app:

  • Leaderboard synchronization: Username, Discipline Score, and streak data are used to display public rankings.
  • Matchmaking: Discipline Score and combat history are used to find fair opponents for battles.
  • AI habit verification: Photos are analyzed by AI to confirm habit completion and deleted immediately after.
  • Progress tracking: Habit, addiction, and streak data power your personal progress dashboard.
  • App improvement: Anonymized, aggregated usage patterns help us fix bugs and improve features.

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it beyond what is needed for app functionality.

4. Sensitive Data - Habits and Addictions

Addiction tracking data and habit logs are treated as sensitive personal information. This data is never shared with third parties, never used for advertising, and never disclosed except as required by law.

Addiction streak data is stored encrypted and is only accessible to you. No other user can see which addictions you track. Only aggregate streak counts (e.g., "47-day streak") may appear in battle contexts, without revealing the addiction type.

5. Verification Photos

When you submit a verification photo:

  • The photo is transmitted securely to our AI processing service.
  • It is analyzed immediately to confirm or reject the habit completion.
  • The photo is not stored permanently. It is deleted within 24 hours of processing.
  • Photos are never shared with other users or third parties.
  • Photos are never used to train AI models without your explicit consent.

6. Authentication via OAuth

Stay Hard uses OAuth-based authentication (Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google) provided via Manus. We do not store your passwords. Authentication tokens are managed by the OAuth provider and used only to verify your identity at login.

7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We only share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Infrastructure and AI processing services operating under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements: If required by applicable law or governmental authority in Spain or the EU.

No advertising networks. No data brokers. No third-party analytics that identify you personally.

8. Data Retention

  • Account and profile data: Retained until account deletion.
  • Habit and streak data: Retained until account deletion.
  • Verification photos: Deleted within 24 hours of processing.
  • Combat and leaderboard data: Retained up to 12 months after account deletion for integrity auditing, then permanently deleted.
  • Anonymized analytics: May be retained indefinitely in aggregate, non-identifiable form.

10. Age Requirements

Stay Hard is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 17. If we become aware a user is under 17, their account and data will be deleted immediately.

11. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption in transit and encrypted storage at rest. We will notify affected users in the event of a data breach as required by applicable law.
